Grilled Chicken

Juicy. Flavorful. Perfectly grilled.

1 hour Medium difficulty

Ingredients

  • 350 gm chicken
  • 1/2 tsp ginger paste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt as required
  • 2 tsp virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup hung curd
  • 1/2 tsp garlic paste
  • 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garam masala

Instructions

1

Marinate the Chicken

Wash the chicken thoroughly and pat dry. In a large bowl, mix all the marinade ingredients (ginger paste, garlic paste, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, black pepper, garam masala, salt, and olive oil) with the hung curd to form a smooth paste. Coat the chicken pieces evenly with this marinade.

2

Refrigerate

Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 8 hours, or preferably overnight. This allows the flavors to penetrate deeply into the chicken.

3

Preheat the Grill

Preheat your grill to 190°C (375°F). Make sure the grates are clean and lightly oiled to prevent sticking.

4

Grill the Chicken

Place the marinated chicken pieces on the grill. Cook for about 30 minutes, then flip and cook for another 30 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 75°C (165°F). Baste occasionally with any remaining marinade during the first 15 minutes of cooking.

5

Rest and Serve

Remove from grill and let rest for 5 minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the meat.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this grilled chicken with fresh salad, roasted vegetables, or naan bread. Pairs well with mint chutney or yogurt sauce. For a complete meal, serve with lemon rice or quinoa.

Notes

  • For best results, use bone-in chicken pieces as they stay juicier during grilling.
  • If you don't have a grill, you can bake in the oven at 200°C (400°F) for 40-45 minutes.
  • For extra smoky flavor, add a small piece of charcoal to the marinade for 10 minutes before grilling (remove before cooking).
